Our Mission


We believe in the power of women to change their own lives – and the world around them – for the better. When women work together, there’s nothing we can’t do. But while talent is ubiquitous, opportunity is not. That’s where we make a difference. About Naari


At Naari, our aim is to enhance the financial independence of women and integrate them into the mainstream money related matters. We believe that in the 21 st century, for India to become a world leader equal participation of women is required and the real key to uplift women lies in their financial literacy. Thus, we came up with a solution, a portal uniquely designed for women- the Naari.

It not just helps the women understand the basic workflow of legal documents and Banking solutions, but our key feature the “Gullak” scheme acts as a game changer for all investment needs. We also provide an e-market center for women entrepreneurs, Self-Help Groups (SHG) and women operated MSMEs to register and advertise their products. The progress of a woman leads to the development of a complete family. With this portal, we hope to bring a positive trend and make a difference by financial inclusion of women into our society.





Services We Provide

Gullak

Gullak is digital form of commitee. Gullak is a central Bank Account where any woman can add money. The added money will be invested in the market.

Open a Bank Account

Woman can open their bank account by talking with the bank sales representative through video call or voice call and if satisfied they can share their details to the bank for opening their account in that respective bank.

HardCash to DigiCash

The woman will provide their hard cash to the shopkeeper, the shopkeeper will click the photo of the note and the ML model will detect whether it is fake or not and inform the value of note. The amount will then be transferred from the shopkeeper to woman's bank account using upi.

Be Vocal for Local

Every MSME needs promotion to get its product to the desired audience. Posters, wall paintings and TV ads do not provide the review of the product, thus having vocal representatives increases the credibility of product. We are presenting a platform to MSME where they can hire women from local regions to promote their products & women will receive credits and incentives for promoting the product.
