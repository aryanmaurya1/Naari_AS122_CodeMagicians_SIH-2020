Advertise your MSME:

Is your business affected by the recent Covid-19 pandemic ?

Are you facing a shortage of man-power ?

Do you wish to reach out to a larger customer base ?

Are you interested in connecting with people who can be vocal about your product ?

If your answer to all these questions is Yes, then you are certainly at the right place.

MSMEs form a major part of our economy and are integral to our country’s development. The Covid-19 situation has slowed the industry and the women run MSMEs are the hardest hit. We at Naari are determined to help your MSME grow and with this portal we provide you a platform to promote your product in our e-market and get valuable stats such as likes, interests related to the product. It also enables the MSMEs to post the job requirements and connect to candidates thus saving a large portion of your valuable time. The uniqueness of this platform is its ability for the MSMEs to connect with the people who are interested to become a representative of their product in a particular area thus supporting the notion Vocal for Local.

How It Works ?

1. Signup or Login to our MSME portal.

2. Choose whether to add your product in the market or Hire women candidates.

3. To promote your product in the e-market, submit all details of your product and then add it into the market.

4. For candidate hiring, describe the nature and job description and then post the job.
