Local for Vocal

Even a single small purchase can lead to a smile on local vendors and businesses!!!.

Everyday we come across so many products and services that leave an impact on us but we rarely appreciate their work. . This has led to foreign investments capture a large portion of Indian market disrupting lakhs of businesses and leading to unemployment.

To see our nation as the World Leader again, it is evident that we need to strengthen our industry, particularly the MSMEs and the unorganized market sectors. This can be done when people of a particular area start appreciating their own indigenous markets and helping them grow their business. Keeping, the values of harmony and trust, we provide you with a platform to become Vocal about a local product you viewed Inside the marketplace. It will help the local vendors and business to improve the reach and their hard work gets equal respect as that of a MNC. The person can then connect with the owner and provide their interest of becoming representative .

How It Works ?

1. Once you choose to become a vocal for local, search for product from our e-market place.

2. Now enter all your basic details and submit your interest.

2. The MSME or the product owner will review your application.

4. On getting selected, you become the face of that MSME in your area.
