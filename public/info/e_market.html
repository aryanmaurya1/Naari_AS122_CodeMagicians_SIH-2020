E-Market

As the maverick saying, "women prove to run businesses better", we have designed a digital marketplace for women to Buy and Sell anything from anywhere at no extra cost. With the changing scenario, going to market physically is challenging and risky. This e-market is designed to facilitate the women entrepreneurs, women self-help-groups and those who wish to start their journey by showcasing the world their talent can drive their businesses. It allows them to list their product and cater to a wide range of customers beyond physical restrictions. Women entreprenuers can observe the reach of their product both location-wise and age-wise. It also provides the feature to find a local vocal (local representative to promote their product).

How It Works ?

For Women Entrepreneurs and Business :-
  • Signup or Login to our portal.
  • Promote your product to market.
  • See insights of your product from your MSME Dashboard
For Buyers
  • One can directly visit the marketplace.
  • Choose the product of their choice
  • Confirm details and proceed to checkout.
  • Become a vocal of your favourite product.
