Open a bank account

Do you wish to open a bank account but confused which bank to opt for? Let go of all your worries. The Power is now in your hands, Get to know your Bank before actually opening account

How It Works ?

1. A woman needs to login or signup in our portal.

2. Once successful login, a number of banks are listed in the dashboard.

3. The woman can then select a bank and the assigned bank representative will answer all her concerns and queries over voice call or email.

4. The identity of the woman will remain anonymous during the conversation.

5. Once, the woman is satisfied with a bank’s product, she can then proceed to share her details to open the bank account in that respective bank.
