At Naari, our aim is to enhance the financial independence of women and integrate them into the mainstream money related matters. We believe that in the 21 st century, for India to become a world leader equal participation of women is required and the real key to uplift women lies in their financial literacy. Thus, we came up with a solution, a portal uniquely designed for women- the Naari.
